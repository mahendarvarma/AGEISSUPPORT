About AGEIS SUPPORT

Empowering lives with care, dignity, and support across Australia

Our Mission

We believe care and kindness should be accessible to everyone, regardless of age, ability, or circumstance.

AGEIS SUPPORT was founded on the principle that every Australian deserves access to quality care services. We bridge the gap between those who need help and the trusted service providers who can offer it, making the process simple, safe, and emotionally comforting.

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy and understanding.

Trust

We maintain the highest standards of safety and reliability.

Community

We foster connections that strengthen our communities.

Dignity

We respect and preserve the dignity of every individual we serve.

Our Vision

Support for every stage of life through compassion and connection.

We envision a future where no Australian faces their challenges alone. Our platform serves as a digital helping hand, connecting elderly citizens, people with disabilities, and individuals struggling with mental health to the support they need.

Government Partnership

We assist users in accessing NDIS and Aged Care services, making government-funded support more accessible and understandable.

NDIS Support

We help individuals with disabilities navigate the National Disability Insurance Scheme, connecting them with registered providers and ensuring they receive the support they're entitled to.

  • NDIS plan management assistance
  • Provider matching and coordination
  • Plan review support
  • Service quality monitoring

Aged Care Services

We facilitate access to government-funded aged care services, including home care packages and residential care options.

  • Home care package coordination
  • Residential care placement assistance
  • Assessment support
  • Family consultation services

Mental Health Programs

We connect individuals with Medicare-funded mental health services and community support programs.

  • Mental health plan coordination
  • Therapy service matching
  • Crisis support connections
  • Recovery program assistance

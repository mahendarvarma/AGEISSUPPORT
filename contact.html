Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Booking is simple and free! You can book online through our booking form, call us at 1800 AGEIS, or email us. We'll match you with qualified service providers in your area.

What government funding is available?

We help you access NDIS funding, Aged Care packages, and Medicare rebates for mental health services. Our team can assist you in understanding and applying for these programs.

How quickly can I get service?

For urgent needs, we can arrange services within 24 hours. Standard bookings are typically scheduled within 1-2 weeks. Emergency services are available 24/7.

Are your service providers qualified?

Yes! All our service providers are fully qualified, background-checked, and registered with relevant professional bodies. We maintain the highest standards of care and safety.

What areas do you service?

We provide services across Australia, with strong coverage in major cities and regional areas. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific location.

Can I change or cancel my booking?

Absolutely! You can modify or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled service. We understand that needs can change, and we're flexible to accommodate you.
